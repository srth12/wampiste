Welcome to ISTE

We drive technology that drives the world!

What distinguishes an institution of higher learning is the repertoire of technical acumen it commands. Equally important is the enabling environment that not only fosters percolation of knowledge from this reservoir, but also inspires students to delve deep into this reservoir in pursuit of knowledge. While National Institute of Technology Calicut (NITC) has no dearth of any of these that render it a distinguished temple of learning, contributing its mite in further promoting these enabling factors is its own chapter of Indian Society for Technical Education (ISTE).

Headquartered in New Delhi, ISTE has the distinction of being a fraternity of students spread across premier technical education institutes in the country, including the top IITs. Patronised by the faculty, administration and students alike, ISTE is devoted to the cause of promoting interaction among its chapters aimed at widening the outlook of its members. It also hosts events aimed at challenging its members to bring out the best in them, be it on theory or practice and lock horns with the best among peers.

Imbibing the spirit of its parent, ISTE chapter at NITC has launched many ambitious projects, marveled by counterparts elsewhere. Outstanding among them is the launching, for the 1st time in the annals of NITC, a well-acclaimed newsletter, Oracle, since rechristened, Inquisite. Issue by issue it has been showcasing the marvels of technology, still to be resolved riddles of science, latest launches, what is in the incubator etc. Not to be outwitted, it also features humour, anecdotes, brain teasers and FAQs that enliven the readers and keep them further engrossed.

Yet another feather in the cap of this Chapter is its own scholarship instituted in the name of eminent scientist and former President of the country, Dr. A.P.J. Abdul Kalam. True to its name, the scholarship aims to encourage hidden gems among students of NITC with potential to achieve pre-eminence in the field of science and technology. The rigorous process of layered filtration and elimination comprises of written tests, power-point presentations, group discussions and interviews aimed at wholesome evaluation spanning beyond technical knowledge and skills, to encompass aptitude, attitude and communication skills so that the truly best among the several meritorious is discovered and rewarded.

Events of immense pride and satisfaction to the Chapter are Spectrum and iCON, sponsored through wholehearted team effort of its members. While the former is an inter-branch competition for the 1st years to face a battery of technical challenges and emerge victorious to claim Spectrum trophy, the latter is an inter-collegiate competition hosted by the Chapter, where competitors from institutions across the country converge at NITC to flaunt their technical skills and establish supremacy. The thrill of organizing these events, the excitement of participation and the aura surrounding these events are what have to be experienced than described.
