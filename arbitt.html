Arbitt


ARBITT , is considered as one of the most successful events of ISTE. The name may sound somewhat arbitrary but actually it is not so. It's a therapy which will brush your grey matter. In this event an arbitrary theme is taken and an aptitude test is conducted on that theme. The themes range through a variety of topics like cryptography, constellations, how stuff works, wars, paradoxes, etc. Attractive cash prizes also await the winners of each ARBITT. During 2010-2011, 6 ARBTT tests were conducted on different areas. Increased restrictions have made it harder and harder for the ISTE team to continue with the event in the academic year 2011-2012.
But, it isn’t over yet!
Plans are on to restart the event in this semester itself. And if all goes well, students can look forward to atleast 2 ARBITT tests this semester. Well if you want to gain something in various fields to hold a firm position in this competitive era, get ready for it!
