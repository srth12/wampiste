Career Workshop '12

Career Workshop, an important & successful event of ISTE, was held on 15th and 16th of September 2012. More than 150 students attended the morning sessions which included Introduction to Placement Skill Enhancement Workshop by Career Launcher and Placement and Internship talks by fourth year seniors. These sessions were quite informative for second and third year students. Mock Exams were conducted in the evening session- Mock GRE by Career Launcher on 15th of September and Mock GATE by GATE Forum on 16th of September. More than 500 students appeared for the exam. ISTE NITC Students’ Chapter would hereby like to thank the members of Career Launcher,

  • Mr. K Rahul (Director of Career Launcher, Calicut Center), for briefing us about the career options after B tech especially, GATE and CAT
  • Mr. Mohd. Ikan (Soft Skill Trainer) , for taking such a good and interactive session on soft skills, and
  • Mr. Ajay Bhagthan , for making us aware of the present GRE Pattern.

We would also like to thank our fourth year seniors Mr.Akshay, Mr.R Venkatesh, Ms. Reshma Babu, Mr. Sandeep, Ms.Ashwini, Ms.Snehal Chauhan, Ms.Yalla Monica , Mr. Shamil & Ms.Tanya Dey for sharing their experience and knowledge with us regarding Placement and Internships. And last but not the Least, we would like to thank the audience for giving such an overwhelming response.
