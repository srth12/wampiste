Abdul Kalam Scholarship


The Abdul Kalam Scholarship (AKS) is the premier scholarship examination conducted by ISTE exclusively for the first years.

Format:
The AKS is divided into three stages, namely, the Written test, Group Discussion (GD) and a Personal Interview (PI).

WRITTEN TEST:
The first round is the screening stage in which the students are tested on subjects, namely, Mathematics, Physics, Chemistry, Computer Science and General Aptitude. It is mandatory for the students to clear a certain cut off in each section in order to proceed to further rounds.


GD:
The Qualifying scholars from the Written test will be asked to discuss on topics given by the panel of judges.The student's knowledge of concepts and his/her communication skills is evaluated in this round. Further shortlisting ensues and successful candidates move on to the next round.


PI:
Now, these AK scholars will have to undergo the PI (Personal Interview). The questions shot at the scholars, by the interviewing panel, will be general questions from which the panel will judge the candidate's perspective of looking at certain situations. Eventually, after all these rounds, we will select candidates for the top three positions and those scholars holding the first, second and third positions will be awarded with cash prizes.
